About

Early career

A frustrated artist. I began my working life in retail banking and financial servcies, a career that spanned some 11 years and took me from the back office processing cheques to independent financial adviser in the UK boom years from the nineties to the noughties. It also baptised me into the world of personal computing as the banking system moved from paper records to electronic
Unable to to condone the economic truth selling of financial servcies products, I found myself looking for an industry where I could leverage the analysis skills I had learned thus far and, like many who find themselves there, I fell into a merchandise planning career.

Recent career

Frustrated with the limitations of both funding and the choice of retail systems, in addition to my 'day job',I found myself striving to improve processes by implementing systems that leveraged off the shelf software.
I made the leap into project management in 2011 and have been able to leverage my broad retail experience and deliver numerous projects ranging from reporting improvements to digital and ecommerce.

Where to from here..

Combining my creative drive, my technical abilities and my thirst to learn something new, I have embarked on a coding journey.
My aims are to master sufficient coding languages to be able to offer 'full stack' experience and to not be limited to living in a particular part of the world. I now find myself a 'stay at home Dad' trying to master the coding languages whilst balancing the demands of two young children. Follow me if you like, I will update my blog when I can
